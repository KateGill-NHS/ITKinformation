COCD_TP146227GB01
ServiceEvent

A template to identify the service being documented

This class represents the main Act, such as a colonoscopy or an appendectomy, being documented.

The ServiceEvent class specializes the act inherent in the ClinicalDocument.code, such as where the ClinicalDocument.code is simply "Procedure Report" and the procedure was a "colonoscopy".

The ServiceEvent shall not conflict with the value inherent in the ClinicalDocument.code.
