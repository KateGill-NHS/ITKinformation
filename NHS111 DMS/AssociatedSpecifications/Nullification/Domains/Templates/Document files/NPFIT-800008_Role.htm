Description: : Recipient Templates
Template Usage Guidance:
When a Sender has access to Spine Directory Services (SDS) one of the following templates are recommended for use:
----*RecipientEntitySDS (COCD_TP145021UK05)
----*RecipientOrganisation (COCD_TP145017UK03)
When a Sender does not have access to Spine Directory Services (SDS) one of the following templates are recommended for use:
----*RecipientOrganizationUniversal (COCD_TP145203GB02)
----*RecipientPersonUniversal (COCD_TP145202UK01)
The following templates are deprecated in this constraint and only included for backwards compatibility with existing systems
----*RecipientEntitySDS (COCD_TP145021UK04)
